Our connection with Sol is severed. The Fold is silent. We've lost contact with Mimir, Alsvin, Muninn, and Hugin. The Brisinger breached the Fold, but we're struggling to re-establish communication with her. We do not yet know why, or for how long. We only know this: for now, Mairee is a secluded island.

Our journey is not over. It has begun. We are prepared. Your pads will receive rationing protocols. Read them. Respect them. And look at the people next to you. They are not your competitors. They are your survival. Our strength is not in our stockpiles. It is in these hands. Our hands, our minds, and our hearts are what makes us strong.

Many of you landed here a cycle ago. But now is the moment when we truly come together. And the rest of the fleet will follow soon. I promise. We arrived. They will come. And we will thrive here. Together.
