Privacy

Who runs this site

This site is operated by Sjoerd Nijland (author, publishing under the name S. Nyland), based in the Netherlands. For questions about your data, see Contact below.

What we collect

  • Account data — when you sign in with Discord, we store your Discord user ID, display name, avatar URL, and email address.
  • Purchase data — when you buy the book, we store the Stripe checkout session ID, the amount, the timestamp, and a status flag (paid / refunded).
  • Comments — anything you post in a paragraph thread is stored under your account.
  • Anonymous usage analytics — page views, which chapters get started, how far people scroll, narration starts/completes. These events are not tied to your account or any personally identifying field.

Payment card details are never seen or stored by this site — they're handled entirely by Stripe.

Why we collect it

  • To authenticate you, unlock the chapters you've paid for, and let you download your EPUB.
  • To attribute the comments you leave to you.
  • To understand which parts of the book engage readers, anonymously, so the site can be improved.
  • To fulfill record-keeping obligations under Dutch and EU law (purchase records retained for 7 years).

Legal basis (GDPR)

  • Contract — purchase processing and book delivery.
  • Consent — you sign in with Discord voluntarily; you can sign out and delete your account at any time.
  • Legitimate interest — anonymous usage analytics.
  • Legal obligation — fiscal retention of purchase records.

Who else sees your data

The following sub-processors are used. None receive more data than they need to do their job:

  • Supabase (Supabase Inc.) — database, authentication, and EPUB storage. Data is stored in the EU region.
  • Stripe (Stripe Payments Europe Ltd., Ireland) — payment processing.
  • Discord (Discord Inc.) — OAuth login provider. We receive your public Discord identity at sign-in; we do not access your servers or messages.
  • ElevenLabs (ElevenLabs Inc.) — text-to-speech for the narration feature. Only the chapter text is sent, never your account data.
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.) — static site hosting.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct anything that's wrong.
  • Ask us to delete your account and associated comments (purchase records may be retained for fiscal reasons).
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Withdraw your consent for Discord-linked sign-in at any time by signing out.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Dutch DPA (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

To exercise any of these, email nyland@agnc.cc.

Cookies & local storage

This site uses minimal browser storage: Supabase Auth sets cookies/localStorage entries to keep you signed in across pages, and the reader caches your font-size preference. No tracking pixels, no third-party advertising cookies.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
